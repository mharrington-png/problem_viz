How Chomp works: Players alternate removing a square and everything above/right of it. Whoever is forced to eat the ☠ poison (bottom-left) loses.

Key insight: Player 1 always has a winning strategy on any rectangular board larger than 1×1 — but we often can't say what it is! This is a famous non-constructive proof.

N-position = Next player wins  |  P-position = Previous player wins (current player loses)
