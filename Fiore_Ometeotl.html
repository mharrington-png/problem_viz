Interactive Chomp Strategy Visualizer

Chomp is a two-player mathematical strategy game. Clicking a chocolate square removes that square and every square below and to the right. The navy blue poisoned square in the top-left corner loses the game for whoever is forced to eat it. This visualization highlights winning and losing positions, optimal moves, and game-tree structure.

How Strategy Works

A position is winning if the current player can move to a losing position. A position is losing if every move gives the opponent a winning position.
