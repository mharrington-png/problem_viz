Expected value
E[X] = Σ xᵢ · P(xᵢ)

Each outcome's value weighted by its probability, summed over all outcomes.
Example: If drawing a ♠ pays $4 and others pay $0, E[X] = 4 × P(♠) = 4 × 0.25 = $1.00
